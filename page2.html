Concerns about Artificial Intelligence

Malicious Intent
Loss of Control
Fixation On Task

Malicious Intent

When the robot is created with AI, the programmer can create any parameters he wishes and with an increasingly technological world, blackhats can override these commands to program malicious intent, or harm towards humans.

Loss of Control

AI will cost lesser-skilled people their jobs because they will replace many mundane tasks that lower skilled people complete at this time of day. Robots at this time already take spots from humans on assembly lines, and in the future this will only increase. AI also cannot choose an option or make a decision based on morality. A machine that only makes its decisions based on facts will likely make many incorrect calls compared to a human with the ethics and morals to help guide their decision.

Fixation on Task

With a set of hard programmed controls inbuilt into its systems, its clear that robots once set to a specific task are dedicated to the premise of fulfilling it. Once the program has been written, however the AI algorithm sees it most efficient to complete that task, it will be done in that manner. The negative connotation assigned to this is one where the robot is programmed for malicious purposes and the most efficient method contains harm to life.
