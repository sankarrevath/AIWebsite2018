Benefits of AI

As artificial intelligence technology continues to improve, more and more applications for artificial intelligence are emerging. Businesses and consumers alike are benefiting from the increased efficiency and flexibility provided by artificial intelligence, and the increased availability of information is priceless for the education and research sectors. Beyond information and data, AI has the potential to advance humanity, both on a societal and personal level.

Concerns about AI

As with any emerging technology, the implementation of artificial intelligence in our society has introduced new concerns and enhanced existing ones. Critics of the adoption of artificial intelligence technology point to loss of control or danger to human life. These valid arguments remind us to consider the realistic implications of any new technology and to take a step back before fully diving into the realm of artificial intelligence.
